About me

After four years in management and customer service, I changed my career into software development inspired by my friends. In the past year I worked on this change happen. I started out mostly self-taught, learning what I could from online resources such as UDEMY codecademy, tutorials, books after I joined a General Assembly course to kick-start my career in the tech industry. I made this jump after carefully considering my future and realising that no matter what subject area or fields I’ve worked in, the tasks that I found most rewarding were those that posed the greatest challenge. I have completed four projects, developing both the frontend and backend, throughout the course. The bootcamp gave me industry standard, full-stack MERN development skills with a strong knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Python.
